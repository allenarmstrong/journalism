The Lesson





In this lesson, you will study and review some elements of strong writing to prepare you for your first draft.

The Assessment




For the assessment, you will take a multiple-choice quiz on elements of strong writing, including subject/verb agreement, pronouns, colons, semicolons, and active sentence structure.

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • make subjects and verbs agree
  • use pronouns, colons, and semicolons properly
  • write using active sentence structure
  • write a grammatically-correct first draft
