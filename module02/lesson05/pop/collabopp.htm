02.05 Collaboration Opportunity

Part One:

To participate in this collaboration, you will use the discussion board and/or email feature in the course. Send a message to one of your classmates, asking for a peer review partner. You should aim to find someone who is currently working on the same lesson. Once you have found someone to work with, you will send each other your writing for review. Do not post any of your work in the discussion area.

Part Two:

Review a classmate's news story about his or her chosen career in Journalism using the Guide for Peer Review.
  1. Be sure to use sensitivity when responding to your peers.
  2. All posts are public and meant to engage in a constructive discussion to learn from one another.

Part Three:

Be sure to complete the Collaboration Reflection Worksheet and your Peer Review. Your collaboration reflection will be evaluated according to the Collaboration Rubric.

Part Four:

Submit both the Collaboration Reflection Worksheet and the Guide for Peer Review in the Assessment area, Segment One Collaboration. Print
