Assignment 02.01 Rubric

Reporter's Notebook Part One

25 points

  Mastery Achieved Approaching Mastery Needs Improvement
Demonstration of Understanding 5–4 points
The student has submitted three sources that demonstrate a thorough understanding of the process of research and searching for a career.		 3–2 points
The student has submitted three sources that demonstrate a basic understanding of the process of research and searching for a career.		 1–0 points
The student has not submitted three sources that demonstrate an understanding of the material. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Chosen Career 5–4 points
The student has selected a career path and demonstrated clear, thoughtful research into the process of finding a job.		 3–2 points
The student has selected a career path and done basic research about the process of finding a job.		 1–0 points
The student has not selected a career path and/or has not done basic research into the process of finding a job. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Explanation of Sources 5–4 points
The student has clearly explained each source, providing definitive information about why they can be considered credible.		 3–2 points
The student has explained each source, providing simple information about why they can be considered credible. 		1–0 points
The student has not defended the credibility of the sources. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Clarity of Submission 5–4 points
The assignment is easy for the instructor to grade/view. 		3–2 points
The assignment is mostly easy for the instructor to grade/view, with several errors. 		1–0 points
The assignment is difficult or impossible for the instructor to grade/view. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Grammar and Spelling 5–4 points
The student has respected language conventions where necessary. 		3–2 points
The student has respected some language conventions where necessary.		 1–0 points
The student has not respected language conventions where necessary. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
