Assignment

For this assignment you must identify at least three sources that can give you useful information about your chosen career. These will be part of the requirement for your informative news story about careers in journalism. Make sure all of your sources are credible.

In your Reporter’s Notebook complete Part One by listing and explaining the credible sources of valuable information about your topic.

Your instructor will approve your sources or give you feedback on how to improve them.

You do not need to begin writing your news story yet. However, make sure you are keeping track of your ideas and information so you are ready!
