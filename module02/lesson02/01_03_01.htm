The Lesson

In this lesson, you will learn about how we reached the point where American journalism is today, and you’ll take a look at some of the important technologies that have had a major impact on the way we share news. While you explore this information, pay close attention to the different news media. How do you get your news?

The Assignment

For the assignment, you will use your understanding of news media and technology to search for a news story that has used technology in an interesting way. Maybe you’ll find a newspaper article that includes a fantastic photograph. Maybe you’ll find a radio segment that mixes in unusual sounds. It’s up to you!

Sneak Peek at the Assignment

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • identify various types of available media
  • analyze the evolution of a media type
