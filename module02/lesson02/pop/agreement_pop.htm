Subject/Verb Agreement Refresher

The reason I bought all of these toys and gifts (is/are) that I have several cousins with birthdays in the same month.

What is the subject of this sentence? Is it reason, or is it toys and gifts? To determine the correct answer, you need to look at what the sentence is truly about. Is the person trying to give special information about the toys and gifts, or is the person trying to explain the reason?
