The Lesson





In this lesson, you practice writing a lead for a news story. You will use your preliminary research to help you develop your lead, and you will use this opening sentence to introduce a news story about your chosen career in journalism.

The Assignment




For the assignment, you will use your understanding of the inverted pyramid, as well as the 5Ws and one H, to write a lead for a news story about your chosen career in journalism.

You’ll explain your findings in your Reporter’s Notebook. Take a look at the specific assignment instructions below.
