Assessment

For this assessment, you will create your own definition of a journalist. You should write your definition in your own words, and you should identify three qualifications that a person must meet in order to be a journalist by your standards.

Research modern journalists and find at least one who matches your definition. Explain what makes him or her a true journalist according to the qualifications you have set out.

Your response should be at least one paragraph long, and you must cite any sources you use. Submit your work to your instructor when you are done.
