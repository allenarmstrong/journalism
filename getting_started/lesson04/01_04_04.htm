Types of Assessments (continued)

Project-Based Submission

A project-based submission is an assignment that has several steps and requires research. Be sure to look over the rubric and check the quality of your work before you submit each assignment.

Discussion-Based Assessments

Discussion-Based Assessments are conducted routinely between the instructor and the student. These must be completed free of any distraction, or influence from others. This is also a time to ask your instructor questions.

Collaboration

You will be given the opportunity to work with your fellow classmates and have deeper conversations concerning class issues and topics. Learn more here. Select the buttons below to learn more about Collaboration.

Synchronous Collaboration

In some cases, synchronous collaboration opportunities will be provided. The opportunities will allow you to interact with your classmates online, in real-time, or by using Web 2.0 tools.

Asynchronous Collaboration

You may also have the opportunity to participate in an asynchronous collaboration with one of your classmates. Instructions on how to participate are provided in the course.
