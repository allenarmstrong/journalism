Types of Assessments

Multiple Choice Quizzes or Exams

In these types of exams, you will choose one correct answer from the choices given. To be successful, make sure to read each selection carefully before answering.

Short Answer Responses

Some questions will require you to write a short answer. To be successful, be sure to write a thorough response using complete sentences.

Graphic Organizer

This is a document you will download, and then type or write your ideas into. You will need to save the organizer to your computer or drive before submitting it.

Essays

In this course, you will be writing different types of essays and articles. Be sure to look over the rubric and check the quality of your work before you submit each assignment.
