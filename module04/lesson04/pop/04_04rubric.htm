Collecting Evidence

25 points

  Mastery Achieved Approaching Mastery Needs Improvement
Selection of Sources 5–4 points

The student has selected five credible sources to support his or her argument.

 3–2 points

The student has selected five sources to support his or her argument.

 1–0 points

The student has not selected five sources to support his or her argument. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Explanation of Examples 5–4 points

The student has thoroughly explained the sources and provided a specific example from each that is clearly related to the topic.

 3–2 points

The student has explained the sources and provided an example from each.

 1–0 points

The student has not explained the sources or provided an example from each. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
5Ws and 1H 5–4 points

The student has identified one of the 5Ws and one H for each example, and it is clear how the example answers the question.

 3–2 points

The student has identified one of the 5Ws and one H for each example.

1–0 points

The student has not identified one of the 5Ws and one H for each example. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
