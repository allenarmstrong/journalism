The Lesson





Once you’ve selected a topic for your editorial, it’s time to do some research. This is one of the most important parts of writing an editorial because it helps you make sure you sound knowledgeable about your topic!

The Assignment




For the assignment, you will identify credible sources for your editorial and find specific evidence from those sources that you plan to use.

You will include your answers in your Editorial Notebook. Take a look at the specific assignment instructions below.

Sneak Peek at the Assignment

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • identify credible sources
  • find evidence to support the argument in your editorial writing
