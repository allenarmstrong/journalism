Editorial Notebook Part One

Select one topic from the three options below. You will use this topic later when you write your own opinion piece. For this lesson, you will simply provide a short statement about your claim and counterclaim.

Remember: you must do some research to find a true counterclaim for your argument. Spend some time researching these topics before you decide which one interests you most.

Topics (choose one):

  • The future of news reporting
  • Businesses that collect information about customers online
  • Countries that face debates about net neutrality
  1. Which topic have you selected?
  2. Briefly explain the issues involved in your topic.
  3. In two to three sentences, state your claim.
  4. In two to three sentences, state your counterclaim. This must demonstrate some researched reporting to show you are informed about the topic.
