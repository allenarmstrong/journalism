Assignment 04.02 Rubric

Claim, Counterclaim, and Rebuttal

25 points

  Mastery Achieved Approaching Mastery Needs Improvement
Selection of Topic 5–4 points

The student has selected a topic from the list of topics provided. The student has included a brief explanation of the reasons for choosing that topic, and these reasons demonstrate an understanding of the issue.

 3–2 points

The student has selected a topic from the list of topics provided. The student has included a brief explanation of the reasons for choosing that topic.

 1–0 points

The student has not selected a topic from the list, or has not included a brief explanation of the topic. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Claim 5–4 points

The student has stated a claim that demonstrates a thorough understanding of the argument.

 3–2 points

The student has stated a claim that demonstrates a basic understanding of the argument.

 1–0 points

The student has not stated a claim that demonstrates any understanding of the argument. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Counterclaim 5–4 points

The student has written a researched counterclaim that demonstrates a thorough understanding of the counterargument.

 3–2 points

The student has written a researched counterclaim that demonstrates a basic understanding of the counterargument.

1–0 points

The student has not written a counterclaim that demonstrates any research or understanding of the counterargument. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Clarity of Submission 5–4 points
The assignment is easy for the instructor to grade/view. 		3–2 points
The assignment is mostly easy for the instructor to grade/view, with several errors. 		1–0 points
The assignment is difficult or impossible for the instructor to grade/view. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Grammar and Spelling 5–4 points
The student has respected all language conventions where necessary. 		3–2 points
The student has respected some language conventions where necessary.		 1–0 points
The student has not respected language conventions where necessary. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
