Connotations

  • Hunting requires knowledge of the appropriate tools needed to kill and field dress animals while you are out in the woods.
  • Hunting is essentially the murderous act of stomping into an animal’s home uninvited and destroying whatever you see with man-made acts of violence.
  • Hunting is an excellent way to bond with your friends and family in a calm, natural environment of beauty where you can appreciate majestic animals in their habitats.

Check Your Answer

Positive Connotations:

Hunting is an excellent way to bond with your friends and family in a calm, natural environment of beauty where you can appreciate majestic animals in their habitats.

  • Hunting: Depending on your feelings about this word, your reaction to it might be different from other reactions. It is not a word the writer can count on for a specific emotional response.
  • Excellent: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of success and happiness.
  • Friends: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of community and happiness.
  • Family: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of safety and happiness.
  • Calm: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of relaxation and happiness.
  • Natural: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of health and cleanliness.
  • Environment: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of beauty and community.
  • Beauty: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of attractiveness and joy.
  • Appreciate: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of acceptance and beauty.
  • Majestic: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of amazement and respect.
  • Animals: This is a common word that may or may not have an emotional connection with some audiences.

Negative Connotations:

Hunting is essentially the murderous act of stomping into an animal’s home uninvited and destroying whatever you see with man-made acts of violence.

  • Hunting: Depending on your feelings about this word, your reaction to it might be different from other reactions. It is not a word the writer can count on for a specific emotional response.
  • Murderous: This word is generally associated with negative feelings of violence and fear.
  • Stomping: This word is generally associated with negative feelings of rudeness and destruction.
  • Home: This word is generally associated with pleasant feelings of safety and happiness. It is one that a writer can count on for a positive emotional response, and it has been used here to show the animal has a home instead of a more neutral “habitat.”.
  • Uninvited: This word is generally associated with negative feelings of rudeness and imposition.
  • Destroying: This word is generally associated with negative feelings of violence and devastation.
  • Whatever: This word is generally associated with negative feelings of a lack of caring or thought.
  • Man-made: This word has been used to show a contrast between the natural environment of the animals and the harsh machines of man. In this case, it is a word that a writer can count on for a negative emotional response.
  • Violence: This word is generally associated with negative feelings of pain and destruction.

Neutral connotations:

Hunting requires knowledge of the appropriate tools needed to kill and field dress animals while you are out in the woods.

*NOTE* Because words like “you,” “in,” “is,” and “a” are simple parts of speech, they are generally considered neutral words with no strong connotations.
