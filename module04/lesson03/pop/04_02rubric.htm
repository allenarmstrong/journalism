Evaluate a Speaker

25 points

Selection of Topic 5–4 points

The student has explained his or her answers regarding the evaluation of the speech. The explanation demonstrates a thorough understanding of logos, pathos, ethos, claim, counterclaim, and rebuttal.

 3–2 points

The student has explained his or her answers regarding the evaluation of the speech. The explanation demonstrates a basic understanding of logos, pathos, ethos, claim, counterclaim, and rebuttal.

 1–0 points

The student has not explained his or her answers. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Appeals 5–4 points

The student has identified three appeals in the speech, marking clear examples of logos, pathos, and ethos.

 3–2 points

The student has identified three appeals in the speech. The selections may confuse logos, pathos, and ethos, but all three are present.

 1–0 points

The student has not identified three appeals in the speech. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
