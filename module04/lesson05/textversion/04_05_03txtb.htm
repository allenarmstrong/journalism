04.05 Sentence Structures—Text Version

Simple Sentence

Contains one independent clause: Chen loves to argue.

Compound Sentence

Contains two or more independent clauses: Chen loves to argue, but Cheyenne wants everyone to agree.

Complex Sentence

Contains one independent clause and at least one dependent clause: Although Chen loves to argue, Cheyenne wants everyone to agree.

Compound-Complex Sentence

Contains at least two independent clauses and at least one dependent clause: Although Chen loves to argue, Cheyenne wants everyone to agree, and I agree with Cheyenne.
