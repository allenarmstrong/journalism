The Lesson





With your research in hand, you’re ready to shape your argument. There are many kinds of journalists who focus on opinion writing in their careers. In this lesson, you’ll decide what kind of opinion piece you want to write and you’ll work on polishing your rebuttal to complete your rough draft.

The Assignment




For the assignment, you will identify what type of opinion piece you are writing, and you will complete a rough draft.

You’ll include your answers in your Editorial Notebook. Take a look at the specific assignment instructions below.

Sneak Peek at the Assignment

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • select a writing style
  • clarify your rebuttal
  • vary sentence structure
  • complete a rough draft
