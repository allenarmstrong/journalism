Sentence Structure

Journalists strive to keep their news stories clear and to the point. One way to do this is to vary your sentence structure.

Your writing will become most effective when you use several different types of sentences. Some should be short, while others should be longer and more complex. There are four basic types of sentences you can use in your writing. As you write your rough draft, try to use them all.

Chen loves to argue, but Cheyenne wants everyone to agree.

Contains two or more independent clauses.

Although Chen loves to argue, Cheyenne wants everyone to agree.

Contains one independent clause and at least one dependent clause.

Although Chen loves to argue, Cheyenne wants everyone to agree, and I agree with Cheyenne.

Contains at least two independent clauses and at least one dependent clause.
