Assignment 04.05 Rubric

Write Your Argument

25 points

  Mastery Achieved Approaching Mastery Needs Improvement
Demonstration of Understanding 5–4 points

The student has completed a 350-word first draft, demonstrating a strong understanding of opinion writing.

3–2 points

The student has completed a 350-word first draft, demonstrating a basic understanding of opinion writing.

 1–0 points

The student has not completed a 350-word first draft, or has demonstrated no understanding of opinion writing. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Claim and Counterclaim 5–4 points

The student has included a claim and counterclaim that are entertaining, informative, and show fairness to both sides.

 3–2 points

The student has included a claim and counterclaim.

 1–0 points

The student has not included a claim and counterclaim. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Rebuttal 5–4 points

The student has written a rebuttal that responds satisfactorily to the counterclaim.

 3–2 points

The student has written a rebuttal.

1–0 points

The student has not written a rebuttal. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Clarity of Submission 5–4 points
The assignment is easy for the instructor to grade/view. 		3–2 points
The assignment is mostly easy for the instructor to grade/view, with several errors. 		1–0 points
The assignment is difficult or impossible for the instructor to grade/view. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Grammar and Spelling 5–4 points
The student has respected all language conventions where necessary. 		3–2 points
The student has respected some language conventions where necessary.		 1–0 points
The student has not respected language conventions where necessary. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
