The Lesson





Being able to craft a strong argument is a special skill. Certain journalists use this skill in their editorial writing to appeal to audiences on several levels. It's important to understand how argumentative writing works so you can use it yourself—and also so you can never be fooled by it!

The Assessment




For the assessment, you will complete a multiple-choice quiz to demonstrate your understanding of the topics in this lesson.

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to identify the different appeals in an argument.
