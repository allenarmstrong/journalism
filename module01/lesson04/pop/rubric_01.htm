The student has written and strongly defended a clear and insightful definition of journalism.		 3–2 points
The student has written and defended a clear definition of journalism.		 1–0 points

Modern Journalist 5–4 points
The student has identified a modern journalist and explained clearly how the professional matches his or her definition of journalism.		 3–2 points
The student has identified a modern journalist and partially explained how the professional matches his or her definition of journalism. 		1–0 points
The student has not identified a modern journalist. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.

The assignment is easy for the instructor to grade/view. 		3–2 points

The assignment is difficult or impossible for the instructor to grade/view. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.

The student has respected language conventions where necessary. 		3–2 points
