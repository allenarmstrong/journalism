Sneak Peek at the Assignment 01.04

Who Are Journalists?

For this assignment, you will create your own definition of a journalist. You should:
  • write your definition in your own words
  • identify three qualifications that a person must meet in order to be a journalist by your standards
  • research modern journalists and find at least one who matches your definition
  • explain what makes him or her a true journalist according to the qualifications you have set out
  • write a one paragraph response, citing any sources you use
Submit your work to your instructor when you are done.
