The Lesson





In this lesson, you will learn about the First Amendment of the Constitution and what it means for American journalism. You will also take a look at questions of freedom of speech to gain a better understanding of the tricky issues that can come up!

The Assessment




For the assessment, you will complete a multiple-choice quiz to demonstrate your understanding of the topics in this lesson.

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • analyze and understand the First Amendment
  • identify difficult questions regarding freedom of speech
