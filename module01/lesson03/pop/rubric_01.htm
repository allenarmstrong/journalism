Assignment 01.03 Rubric

Journalism Today

25 points

  Mastery Achieved Approaching Mastery Needs Improvement
Demonstration of Understanding 5–4 points
 The student has submitted a complete assignment that demonstrates a clear understanding of the use of technology in journalism today.		 3–2 points
The student has submitted an assignment that demonstrates a partial understanding of the use of technology in journalism today.		 1–0 points
The student has submitted an assignment that demonstrates no understanding of the topic. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Selection of News Story 5–4 points
 The student has selected an appropriate news story that uses technology in a unique or interesting way.		 3–2 points
The student has selected an appropriate news story that uses technology.		 1–0 points
The student has not selected an appropriate news story, or the story does not use technology. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Explanation of Technology 5–4 points
 The student has clearly explained the use of technology in the selected news story. The explanation is thorough and thoughtful.		 3–2 points
The student has explained the use of technology in the selected news story. 		1–0 points
The student has not explained the use of technology in the selected news story, or the explanation is under five sentences long. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Clarity of Submission 5–4 points
 The assignment is easy for the instructor to grade/view. 		3–2 points
The assignment is mostly easy for the instructor to grade/view, with several errors. 		1–0 points
The assignment is difficult or impossible for the instructor to grade/view. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Grammar and Spelling 5–4 points
 The student has respected language conventions where necessary. 		3–2 points
The student has respected some language conventions where necessary.		 1–0 points
The student has not respected language conventions where necessary. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
