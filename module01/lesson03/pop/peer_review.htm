01.03 Guide for Peer Review

As you read and review your classmate's work, answer the following questions with as much detail as possible. Your feedback will help your classmate improve his or her writing.


Reviewer (first and last name): __________________________________________________


  1. Was the answer to the question “How can technology make news more engaging and understandable?” highly effective? Please explain.
  2. Did the chosen story show technology being used in an interesting or unique way? Be specific.
