The Lesson





In this lesson, you will learn how to enhance your feature story using technology—while keeping a close eye on ethical practices in multi-media journalism.

The Assessment




For the assessment, you will complete a multiple-choice quiz to demonstrate your understanding of the topics in this lesson.

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use images and technology to enhance your feature story
  • think ethically about visual and audio media in journalism
