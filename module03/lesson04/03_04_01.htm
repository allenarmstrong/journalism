The Lesson





In this lesson, you will learn how to build your feature story so it both engages and informs your audience. You will also draft a satisfying conclusion for your story.

The Assignment




For the assignment, you will complete the first draft of your feature story.

You’ll include your draft and an explanation of your work in your Feature Reporter’s Notebook. Take a look at the specific assignment instructions below.

Sneak Peek at the Assignment

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use details and anecdotes to build your feature story
  • create a conclusion for your feature story
