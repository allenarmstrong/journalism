Assignment 03.04 Rubric

Feature Reporter's Notebook Part Three

25 points

  Mastery Achieved Approaching Mastery Needs Improvement
Demonstration of Understanding 5–4 points

The student has completed a 500-word first draft, demonstrating a strong understanding of feature news writing.

 3–2 points

The student has completed a 500-word first draft, demonstrating a basic understanding of feature news writing.

 1–0 points

The student has not completed a 500-word first draft, or has demonstrated no understanding of feature news writing. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Details and Anecdotes 5–4 points

The student has included details and anecdotes that are entertaining, informative, and hold strong emotional appeal.

 3–2 points

The student has included details and anecdotes.

 1–0 points

The student has not included details and anecdotes. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Lead and Conclusion 5–4 points

The student has written a conclusion that matches the lead, making any necessary revisions to the lead.

 3–2 points

The student has written a clear conclusion to the feature story.

1–0 points

The student has not written a conclusion. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Clarity of Submission 5 points
The assignment is easy for the instructor to grade/view. 		4–3 points
The assignment is mostly easy for the instructor to grade/view, with several errors. 		2–0 points
The assignment is difficult or impossible for the instructor to grade/view. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Grammar and Spelling 5 points
The student has respected all language conventions where necessary. 		4–3 points
The student has respected some language conventions where necessary.		 2–0 points
The student has not respected language conventions where necessary. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
