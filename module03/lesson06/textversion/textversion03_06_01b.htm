Speaker Confidence - Text Version

Drag the items below to the scene to see how they can improve the speaker's confidence.

The presentation begins by showing a scene with a man. He is standing at the front of a room with people sitting around a table, looking at him. He is dressed in jeans and a pull-over sweater, his hands are in his pockets and is looking to the side of the room, avoiding eye contact with other people in the presentation.

Presentation material

Know your stuff
When the presentation material is dragged onto the table the speaker is shown. He is dressed in jeans and a pull-over sweater, his arms are crossed and he is looking slightly toward the crowd. He knows his material and is confident in his work.

A glass of water

Bring water
When the water is dragged onto the table the speaker is shown. He is still dressed in jeans and a pull-over sweater but has unfolded his arms they are now at his side. He is more confident now.

A business suit

Feel good about your appearance
When the business suit is dragged onto the speaker he is shown dressed in the suit. He looks professional and confident, his arms are at his sides as he presents. He has a slight slouch and you may wonder if he is still nervous.

Pink pajamas

Visualize something funny
When the pajamas are dragged onto the scene we see that the speaker has visualized the people attending his presentation are dressed in multi-colored pajamas. One person is holding a stuffed teddy bear. The speaker is smiling at the crowd, has a good posture and appears very confident.

Well done! As you can see, a speaker who prepares to speak and to be calm is a happy speaker!
