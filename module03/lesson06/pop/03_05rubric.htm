Assignment 03.05 Rubric

Feature Reporter's Notebook Part Four

50 points

  Mastery Achieved Approaching Mastery Needs Improvement
Demonstration of Understanding 15–12 points

The student has thoroughly revised the feature story, showing a strong understanding of revision techniques and newswriting style.

11–8 points

The student has revised the feature story, showing a basic understanding of revision techniques.

7–0 points

The student has not properly revised the feature story. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.

Highlighted Changes 10 points

The student has clearly highlighted all places where the draft has been revised.

9–5 points

The student has highlighted at least one place where the draft has been revised.

4–0 points

The student has not highlighted the places where the draft has been revised. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.

Reflection 10–9 points

The student has written five to seven complete sentences reflecting on the revision process. The reflection is thorough and thoughtful.

8–6 points

The student has written five to seven sentences reflecting on the revision process.

5–0 points

The student has not written five to seven sentences reflecting on the revision process. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
