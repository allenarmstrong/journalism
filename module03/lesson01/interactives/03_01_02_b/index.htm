"Be accountable."

Before you make information public that might hurt someone else, ask yourself whether the information is something your audience needs to know. Strive to avoid harming others.

"Minimize harm."

The more informed you are as a journalist, the better job you can do with your reporting.

"Seek the truth and report it as fully as possible."

By keeping yourself apart from organizations that are the subject of the news, you can objectively report on those organizations without raising questions from your audience.

"Act independently."
