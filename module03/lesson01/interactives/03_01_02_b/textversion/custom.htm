03.01 Code of Ethics - Text Version

Slide 1

Image: A man is looking back over something he has written.
Popup: "Be accountable."
Make sure all of your information is clear, correct, and understandable. If it is not, run a correction as soon as you find the problem.

Slide 2

Image: Woman surrounded by microphones, video recorders, and cameras.
Popup: "Minimize harm."
Before you make information public that might hurt someone else, ask yourself whether the information is something your audience needs to know. Strive to avoid harming others.

Slide 3

Image: Person crossing their fingers behind their back.
Popup: "Seek the truth and report it as fully as possible."
The more informed you are as a journalist, the better job you can do with your reporting.

Slide 4

Image: Reflection in a side car mirror of a photographer taking a picture.
Popup: "Act independently."
By keeping yourself apart from organizations that are the subject of the news, you can objectively report on those organizations without raising questions from your audience.
