The Lesson





In this lesson, you will study ethical concerns that journalists face and how to address these concerns in your own reporting. You will also learn how to create a feature news story and use this understanding to select a topic that you would like to cover.

The Assignment




For the assignment, you will select a topic for a feature news story and consider the ethical implications of your report.

You’ll explain your findings in your Feature Reporter’s Notebook. Take a look at the specific assignment instructions below.

Sneak Peek at the Assignment

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • make ethical decisions in reporting
  • plan a feature news story
