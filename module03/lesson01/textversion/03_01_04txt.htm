The Five Ws and One H

The Six Questions You Need For a Complete and Accurate News Report

  • Who was involved?
  • What happpened?
  • When did it take place?
  • Where did it take place?
  • Why did it happen?
  • How did it happen?

Just the Facts

Be sure to answer each question with factual information that can be proven.

Avoid asking questions that lead to "yes" or "no" answers.
Example:
"Did you enjoy the concert?"
-"Yes."

VS.

"What did you think of the concert?"
-"It was amazing to hear the music live. I was on my feet the entire night!"

Use the 5 Ws when:

  • preparing interview questions
  • organizing news stories
  • writing news stories
  • editing new stories
