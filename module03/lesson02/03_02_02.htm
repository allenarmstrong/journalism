Talking Trash

Has anyone ever said anything bad or untrue about you or someone you know? Maybe it was a rumor at school or a nasty comment on social media. Comments like these can be damaging to a person’s reputation, as well as to his or her emotional state.

No Joking Matter

Many people assume it’s safe to joke with friends online. However, there’s a fine line between what’s acceptable and what isn’t.

In 2008 in the United Kingdom, a man named Jeremiah Barber posted an indecent picture on his friend Raymond Bryce’s Facebook wall. The picture was tagged so that nearly a thousand people could view it, and the comment attached made an offensive claim about Bryce.

Not only did Barber end up serving 150 hours of community service for the image he posted, but he was also sued by his former friend for libel—and his friend won. In 2010, Raymond Bryce collected £10,000 (about $16,000 at the time) in damages for defamation of his character.
