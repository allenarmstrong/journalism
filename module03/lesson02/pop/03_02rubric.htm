Assignment 03.02 Rubric

Feature Reporter's Notebook Part Two

50 points

  Mastery Achieved Approaching Mastery Needs Improvement
Demonstration of Understanding 20–17 points
The student has written at least 250 words of a first draft, demonstrating a strong understanding of feature news writing.		 16–12 points

The student has written at least 250 words of a first draft, demonstrating a basic understanding of feature news writing.

 11–0 points

The student has not written at least 250 words of a first draft, or has demonstrated no understanding of feature news writing. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Sources 10–9 points

The student has shown a thorough understanding of the research he or she did for this story.

 8–6 points

The student has shown a basic understanding of the research he or she did for this story.

 5–0 points

The student has not done any research or has not shown an understanding of that research. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Interviews 10–9 points

The student has conducted interviews and used the responses to improve the narrative of the feature story.

 8–6 points

The student has conducted interviews and included them in the feature story.

5–0 points

The student has not conducted interviews or has not included them in the feature story. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Clarity of Submission 5 points
The assignment is easy for the instructor to grade/view. 		4–3 points
The assignment is mostly easy for the instructor to grade/view, with several errors. 		2–0 points
The assignment is difficult or impossible for the instructor to grade/view. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
Grammar and Spelling 5 points
The student has respected all language conventions where necessary. 		4–3 points
The student has respected some language conventions where necessary.		 2–0 points
The student has not respected language conventions where necessary. The student should seek guidance from his or her instructor on this assignment.
